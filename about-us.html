About Sharada PU College

Sri Sharada Pre University College for women, Sullia is the only women`s Educational Institution located on the Mangalore-Mysore State highway. Its governing body Dakshina Kannada Gowda Vidya Sangha was established in 1921 with a view to provide housing for students in rural areas. As the phrase goes` If a girls learns, a school opens`, to provide more opportunities and better education to the girls of rural areas of Sullia, Sri Sharada Girls High School was established by Dakshina Kannada Gowda Vidya Sangha under the leadership of Dr.Kurunji Venkatramana Gowda in 1982.In 1987 the school received grant from Government. Later as a result of farsightedness of Dr.Kurunji Venkatramana Gowda the institution was upgraded to Pre university college by launching Arts department in 1991.
